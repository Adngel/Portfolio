Death by Senshado

   This was a project we developed with Unreal Engine during the weekend of the gamejam Brains Eden 2018, The gam confront two players who controlling their tanks, they must construct rockets collecting pieces in an arena before the oponent. When the rocket is completed, it is launched against the oponent base. The first player who manage to launch 3 rockets, wins the duel.

The prototype were made by a team of five:

  • Two programmers who developed the Unreal blueprints.
  • An artist for the tanks that players control.
  • An artist for the rocket object.
  • And myself, helping artists with other decorative props for the environment and menus.
