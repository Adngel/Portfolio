Kurgaab: And the curse of the prophet

     The goal of this projects, was experience the development of a game constrained to the limitation of a hardware, in this case, the hardware was an emulator of Nintendo Advance. For it, we made a prototype of 2d action adventure game, the adventurer must reach to the top of the towe, fighting with enemies and avoiding traps.   

     The prototype were made by pairs, in my case, my partner worked in most of the programing aspect, while I was supporting with the assets creation, and the code for the traps, HUD and final score. Some of the challenges that I've found in this project were the next:  

  • The size for the sprites  was requried to not be bigger than 16 pixels.
  • The color palette had a special structure, it was an array of 16x16 colors, but each tile, could only use the colors of 1 row, so the palette and the tiles had to be designed with this restriction in mind.
  • Due to the hardware limitations, we had to reduce the number of enemies per level and discard the shadow layer.

     Although modern consoles hasn't got the limitations that the Nintendo Advance had got, this experience helped me to realize in the importance of tests and optimization.
