Preparation Exam Quiz

     This quiz, is a javascript website made for the Dental Tutors group.

Dental Tutors is a small company that work in the creation of didactic materials for a course of Dental nurses, in asociation with the Greenwich university.

This quiz, includes around 700 questions to help students prepare for the "National Examining Board for Dental Nurses" (NEBDN). 

For this assignment, I was given the pictures, images and the questions in text. My work was:

  • Transform the conceptual images into a functional quiz application. (With buttons, score, menus, etc...).
  • Optimize and polish the images for their use in an online environment.
  • Filling the quiz content with the questions and answers provided.
